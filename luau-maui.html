Frequently Asked Questions

Are there vegetarian options?
Yes. Our buffet includes kalua pork, local fish, and vegetarian dishes like poi, laulau, and fresh tropical fruits. Let us know dietary preferences when booking and we'll ensure you're well-fed.
Can children attend?
Absolutely. Kids love the fire show and hula performances. We offer kids' pricing and family seating. Keiki (children) menu available with milder dishes. Open bar for adults only, of course.
What's the dress code?
Casual but nice. Aloha shirts, sundresses, casual slacks are perfect. No beachwear or overly casual attire. Sunset can be breezy—bring a light shawl or jacket. Flip-flops are fine.
What's the best time to go?
Year-round, but sunset times vary. Winter luaus happen earlier (around 5-5:30pm), summer luaus later (6-6:30pm). Any time is magical. Book ahead, especially in peak season (Dec-Mar, Jul-Aug).
