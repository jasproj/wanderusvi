Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe?
Yes. Helicopter tours operate under strict FAA regulations. Our pilots are certified with thousands of flight hours. Helicopters are incredibly safe aircraft with excellent safety records for commercial tours.
Are there age or weight restrictions?
Minimum age is 2 years old. Weight limits are 250 lbs per person for standard tours. Heavier guests or very tall passengers should call ahead—we can accommodate you, but may need a larger aircraft.
Will we fly over active lava?
Depends on season and volcanic activity. In active periods, yes—you'll see steam vents and possibly glowing lava at night. We plan routes based on current volcanic conditions and always prioritize your safety and best views.
Can I take photos?
Absolutely. We include dedicated photo time and large windows on both sides. Your pilot will position for best shots. Bring a camera or use your phone—we'll make sure you get amazing shots of volcanoes, waterfalls, and the island from above.
