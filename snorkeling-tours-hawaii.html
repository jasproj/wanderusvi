Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need snorkel experience?
No! Our guides work with beginners and experienced snorkelers. We start with shallow, calm waters and provide full instruction and gear fit before heading out.
Will we see sea turtles?
Yes. Our tours include a sea turtle sighting guarantee. We know exactly where to find them based on season and tide. If we don't see turtles, your money back.
What if I get seasick?
We use shallow reefs close to shore, so motion is minimal. Eat a light breakfast beforehand and take ginger or dramamine if prone to seasickness. We also provide coolers with water and snacks.
Which island is best for beginners?
Oahu offers the calmest waters and easiest access. Maui and Big Island have equally stunning reefs but sometimes rougher conditions. Ask your guide to match you with the best option for your experience level.
